About Us

Techno-JAM is an online student resource database that allows student and teachers to access the necessary content material needed for completing the high school curriculum. It features topic by topic handout and notes in the form of eBooks provided by teachers within the field. Study guides and videos explaining topics of a practical nature are made available to give students the opportunity to learn at their own pace.

At TechnoJAM.com Students are also able to take short test that provide feedback to ascertain if the content was learned or grasped after completing a session of study and finally teachers are able to retrieve assessment materials that correlates to the topics in the respective syllabi.
